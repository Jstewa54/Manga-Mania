We Are Live!

Manga Mania has been in the works for more than 4 years. Founder and creater of the website "Homer Simpson" first had the idea when it was suggested to him in the popular subreddit "r/stories" to make a website to house his short stories, and bring them to life. It was in the works for years, with minimal progress being made every here and there. It wasnt until Homer's Girlfriend (now wife) Mara pushed him to finish his website, and make his dream of being a manga author come true.

Fast foward 2 and a half years, and a lot has changed. Homer originally started this website to house his short stories turned manga, until he had an idea. Knowing the struggle of trying to become a popular author and the small window of oppurtunity, he decided to make the website for all insiring authors to give them a chance to publish their work and gain a fanbase. With more than 3000 visitors on day one launch, and now over 25,000 visitors every 2 weeks things can get pretty busy. With the support from his friends and fans from "r/stories" Homer had a popular website, but he needed a team that's where we came along. A website like this needs a team, the Manga Mania Team is everygrowing with many positions, ranging from fellow developers, fellow authors to read stories and make sure they abide the TOS, and many more the team is shaping up to look really good. We're happy to finally be up and running and we'll work to make sure this website is up to standards for all our users, check here every friday for weekly updates and upcoming events and competitons.

Thank you for using our website and we hope your visit is everthing you imagined, whether your're here to read, publish, or apply to join the team we hope we can keep you happy.

Story Time!

Manga Mania is looking for aspiring authors and artists to help our content grow. Manga Mania is a non profit, open source website that allows authors and artists to publish their work, giving them the opportunity to grow a fanbase. That's right, we dont want your money. This website is to help you grow in your craft and give you an opportunity to gain a loyal fanbase. We are actively taking submissions for new content, please be patient our moderator team is small and they get loads of submissions daily. Once your work is approved, you will be emailed with the notification and given further steps on how to monitor your content's traction. Visit our about page for more info, and submission info.
